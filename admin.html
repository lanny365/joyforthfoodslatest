⚙️ One-Time Setup Required

To control construction mode from anywhere in the world, you need a free JSONBin account. This stores your site setting online so all visitors see the correct state.

1
Go to jsonbin.io → Create a free account → Click "Create Bin" and keep it private
2
Paste this as the bin content and click Save:
{"construction":false}
3
Create a Read-only Access Key with Bins Read permission, then open site-config.js and paste your Bin ID plus that read key.
4
Paste your Master Key below and click Save & Connect. The key stays only in this browser, while the public site reads from site-config.js.

🚧 Site Construction Mode

Toggle this to show or hide the "Coming Soon" page for all visitors worldwide.

Mode
Checking status…

How it works

  • When Construction Mode is ON, every visitor on any device sees the "Coming Soon" page.
  • When you are logged in as admin, you will see a gold banner — the full site is still visible to you.
  • When Construction Mode is OFF, your full website is visible to everyone worldwide.
