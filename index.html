OUR STORY

Grown with Care,
Crafted with Love

Lifelink Foods was born in 2010 when our founder returned from a trip through the peanut heartlands of Malaysia with one simple belief: great peanuts deserve to be treated with respect — from soil to shelf.

We work hand-in-hand with local farmers, selecting only the finest grade peanuts. Every batch is cleaned, processed, and packed in our facility to ensure freshness reaches your doorstep every single time.

  • ✔️ Premium Quality Ingredients
  • ✔️ Hygienically Processed
  • ✔️ Nutritious and Delicious
  • ✔️ Freshness Guaranteed
  • ✔️ Proudly Made in Nigeria
